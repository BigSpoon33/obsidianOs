**Focus:** Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves  
**Duration:** ~50 minutes  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate-Advanced

---

## Warm-Up (5 min)

- Bodyweight squats: 15 reps
- Leg swings: 10 each direction
- Hip circles: 10 each direction
- Light cardio: 3 minutes

---

## Main Workout

### 1. Squats
**Sets:** 4 | **Reps:** 8-10  
**Rest:** 2 minutes  
**Notes:** Go as deep as mobility allows, keep chest up

### 2. Romanian Deadlifts
**Sets:** 3 | **Reps:** 10-12  
**Rest:** 90 seconds  
**Notes:** Feel the stretch in hamstrings

### 3. Leg Press
**Sets:** 3 | **Reps:** 12-15  
**Rest:** 90 seconds  
**Notes:** Full range of motion, control the negative

### 4. Leg Curls
**Sets:** 3 | **Reps:** 12-15  
**Rest:** 60 seconds  
**Notes:** Squeeze at the top

### 5. Bulgarian Split Squats
**Sets:** 3 | **Reps:** 10-12 each leg  
**Rest:** 60 seconds  
**Notes:** Great for single-leg strength and balance

### 6. Calf Raises
**Sets:** 4 | **Reps:** 15-20  
**Rest:** 45 seconds  
**Notes:** Pause at top and bottom

---

## Cool Down (5 min)

- Quad stretch
- Hamstring stretch
- Hip flexor stretch
- Light walking or cycling

---

## Progress Tracking

Track in daily notes:
- Squat depth improvement
- Weight increases
- Unilateral balance
- DOMS recovery time
