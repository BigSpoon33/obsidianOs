## Directions

1. Bring water and quinoa to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until quinoa is tender and water is absorbed, 15 to 20 minutes.
2. Place vegetable blend in a microwave-safe bowl; microwave until heated through, about 5 minutes. Stir vegetable blend into quinoa.

I'm giving this 4 stars, because although it was super easy and hassle-free (that on its own is worth 5 stars!), the flavor was lacking and bland (3 stars). The veggie blend I used was a frozen Fire Roasted Black Bean blend (fire roasted sweet corn, black beans, red and green poblano peppers). The veggies did provide some flavor, but the recipe definitely needed some additional seasonings. Even adding salt helped a lot. Of course, the more you tweak, the more it defeats the purpose of a simple recipe. Next time I'll use broth to cook the quinoa. This would also be tasty with some minced cilantro.
