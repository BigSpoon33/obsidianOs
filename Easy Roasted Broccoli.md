## ðŸ³ Directions

1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Cut broccoli florets from the stalk.
3. Peel the stalk and slice into 1/4-inch slices.
4. Mix florets and stem pieces with olive oil in a bowl and transfer to a baking sheet; season with salt and pepper.
5. Roast in the preheated oven until broccoli is tender and lightly browned, about 18 to 20 minutes.
6. Serve and enjoy!
