**Focus:** Back, Biceps, Rear Delts  
**Duration:** ~45 minutes

---

## Warm-Up (5 min)

- Band pull-aparts: 20 reps
- Scapular pull-ups: 10 reps
- Dead hangs: 20 seconds
- Light cardio: 2-3 minutes

---

## Main Workout

### 1. Deadlifts
**Sets:** 4 | **Reps:** 6-8  
**Rest:** 2 minutes  
**Notes:** Focus on form, engage lats

### 2. Pull-Ups (or Lat Pulldowns)
**Sets:** 4 | **Reps:** 8-12  
**Rest:** 90 seconds  
**Notes:** Full range of motion

### 3. Barbell Rows
**Sets:** 3 | **Reps:** 10-12  
**Rest:** 90 seconds  
**Notes:** Keep back flat, pull to lower chest

### 4. Face Pulls
**Sets:** 3 | **Reps:** 15-20  
**Rest:** 45 seconds  
**Notes:** High reps, focus on rear delts

### 5. Dumbbell Curls
**Sets:** 3 | **Reps:** 10-12  
**Rest:** 60 seconds  
**Notes:** Control the weight, squeeze at top

### 6. Hammer Curls
**Sets:** 3 | **Reps:** 10-12  
**Rest:** 60 seconds  
**Notes:** Targets brachialis

---

## Cool Down (5 min)

- Lat stretch
- Bicep stretch
- Forearm stretch
- Light walking
