# Bench Press

## Instructions
1. Lie on the bench with your eyes under the bar.
2. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width.
3. Unrack the bar and lower it to your mid-chest.
4. Press the bar back up explosively.

## Form Tips
- Keep your feet planted firmly on the ground.
- Retract your shoulder blades (pinch them together).
- Don't flare your elbows out too wide.
