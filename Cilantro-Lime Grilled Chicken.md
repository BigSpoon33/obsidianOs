## Directions

1. Whisk cilantro, lime juice, garlic salt, and black pepper together in a large glass or ceramic bowl. Add chicken; toss to evenly coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 30 minutes to overnight.
2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
3. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess. Discard remaining marinade.
4. Cook on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until no longer pink at the bone and juices run clear, about 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near but not touching the bone should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

## Cook's Note:

It's not necessary to use a whole chicken. You can use only legs and thighs to save money or whatever your local supermarket has on sale that week! This is great for skirt steak and pork as well!

If you are in a hurry, marinating is not necessary; just toss your chicken in the mixture and place it on the grill.

If you do not have a grill, bake the chicken in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) according to the recipe instructions for about 30 minutes.
